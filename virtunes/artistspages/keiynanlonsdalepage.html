Keiynan Lonsdale

Pop, Hip-Hop, R&B

Keiynan Lonsdale is an Australian actor, singer, and dancer known for his versatility and captivating performances. With his charismatic presence and undeniable talent, Lonsdale has gained recognition in both film and television. He is best known for his roles in the TV series "The Flash" and the film "Love, Simon." Lonsdale has also showcased his musical abilities through his own music releases, blending genres like pop, R&B, and electronic. With his magnetic charm and multifaceted skills, Keiynan Lonsdale continues to make waves in the entertainment industry.
