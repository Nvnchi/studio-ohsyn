Pitbull

Pop, Dancehall, Latin trap, Reggaeton

Pitbull, also known as Mr. Worldwide, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter who is known for his energetic and catchy party anthems. With his signature blend of hip-hop, pop, and Latin influences, Pitbull has achieved international success. Known for his charismatic stage presence and uplifting lyrics, he has released numerous chart-topping hits like "Give Me Everything" and "Timber." Pitbull's music often celebrates life, positivity, and the global party spirit. With his infectious beats and collaborations with other artists, Pitbull continues to bring his unique energy to the music industry. Dale.
