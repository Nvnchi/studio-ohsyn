Disclosure

Dance, Electronic, House

Disclosure is a British electronic music duo consisting of brothers Howard and Guy Lawrence. Their music combines elements of house, garage, and pop, creating infectious and danceable tracks. Known for their intricate production, catchy melodies, and collaborations with various artists, Disclosure has achieved international success. Their breakthrough hit "Latch" featuring Sam Smith catapulted them into the spotlight, and they have since released a string of chart-topping singles and albums. With their innovative sound and ability to seamlessly blend genres, Disclosure has become a prominent force in the electronic music scene.
