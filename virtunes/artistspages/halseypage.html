Halsey

Pop, Electropop, Art Pop, R&B, Indie Pop

Halsey, born Ashley Nicolette Frangipane on September 29, 1994, is an American singer-songwriter known for her powerful voice and introspective lyrics. With her distinctive blend of pop, alternative, and electropop sounds, Halsey has become a prominent figure in the music industry. Her music often delves into personal experiences, mental health, and societal issues, resonating with a wide audience. Halsey's breakout hits like "Bad at Love" and "Without Me" have solidified her status as a chart-topping artist. With her raw emotion and captivating performances, Halsey continues to leave a lasting impact on the music scene.
