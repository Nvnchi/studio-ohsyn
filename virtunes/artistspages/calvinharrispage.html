Calvin Harris

R&B, Soul, Pop, EDM, Electro House, Electropop

Calvin Harris, born Adam Richard Wiles on January 17, 1984, is a Scottish DJ, record producer, and songwriter. With his infectious beats and chart-topping hits, Harris has become a prominent figure in the electronic dance music (EDM) scene. Known for his collaborations with various artists, his music seamlessly combines EDM, pop, and dance genres. Harris has achieved immense success with tracks like "This Is What You Came For" featuring Rihanna and "One Kiss" with Dua Lipa. With his knack for creating catchy and energetic tunes, Calvin Harris has solidified his status as one of the most influential and successful DJs in the industry.
