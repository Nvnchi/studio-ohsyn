Ashnikko

Pop, Indie Pop/rock, Rock, Alt-pop, Hip-hop, Bubblegum Pop, Punk, Electropop

Ashnikko, born Ashton Casey on February 19, 1996, is an American singer, rapper, and songwriter known for her bold and unapologetic style. With her vibrant and eccentric persona, Ashnikko has carved a niche for herself in the music industry. Her music blends elements of pop, hip-hop, and punk, creating a unique and energetic sound. Ashnikko gained attention with her viral hit "Stupid" in 2019, which showcased her fierce attitude and clever wordplay. Since then, she has continued to release captivating tracks and has collaborated with artists like Grimes and Princess Nokia. Ashnikko's fearless approach to music, combined with her boundary-pushing visuals, has solidified her as a rising star with a dedicated fan base.
