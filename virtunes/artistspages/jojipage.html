Joji

R&B, Alternative, Lo-fi, Soul

Joji, born George Miller on September 18, 1992, is a Japanese-Australian singer, songwriter, and producer who has made a name for himself with his unique blend of R&B, alternative, and lo-fi music. Formerly known as the YouTube personality Filthy Frank, Joji transitioned into a successful music career with his introspective and emotive tracks. Known for his soulful vocals and melancholic lyrics, he has amassed a devoted fan base. With hits like "Slow Dancing in the Dark" and "Run," Joji has showcased his ability to create hauntingly beautiful music that resonates with listeners on an emotional level. With his distinct artistic vision and musical versatility, Joji has become a standout figure in the industry.
