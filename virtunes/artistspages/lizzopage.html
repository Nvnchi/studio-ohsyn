Lizzo

Pop, Soul, R&B, Funk-pop

Lizzo is an American singer, rapper, and flutist who has made a significant impact in the music industry. Known for her vibrant personality and powerful voice, Lizzo creates music that combines elements of pop, hip-hop, and soul. With her empowering lyrics and body-positive message, she has become a voice for self-love and acceptance. Lizzo's breakout hits like "Truth Hurts" and "Good as Hell" have resonated with audiences worldwide, earning her critical acclaim and commercial success. With her energetic performances and infectious confidence, Lizzo has become a beloved figure in popular culture.
