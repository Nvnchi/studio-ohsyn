KAYTRANADA

Electronic, Hip-Hop, R&B, Dance, Funk House

Kaytranada, born Louis Kevin Celestin, is a Canadian DJ, producer, and musician known for his unique blend of electronic, hip-hop, and R&B music. With his infectious beats and soulful sound, Kaytranada has garnered critical acclaim and a dedicated following. His debut album, "99.9%," received widespread praise and won the Polaris Music Prize in 2016. Known for his smooth production style and collaborations with various artists, Kaytranada has solidified his status as a prominent figure in the music industry. With his innovative sound and genre-bending approach, he continues to push boundaries and captivate audiences with his groovy and captivating tracks.
