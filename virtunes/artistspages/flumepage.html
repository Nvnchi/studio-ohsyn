Flume

Dance, Electronic, Future Bass

Flume, born Harley Edward Streten on November 5, 1991, is an Australian record producer, musician, and DJ. Known for his innovative electronic sound and experimental approach to music, Flume has made a significant impact on the electronic music scene. His music combines elements of EDM, future bass, and downtempo, creating a distinct and atmospheric sound. Flume gained international recognition with his self-titled debut album, which featured the hit single "Never Be Like You" featuring Kai. With his unique production style and genre-defying sound, Flume continues to push boundaries and captivate audiences with his mesmerizing tracks.
