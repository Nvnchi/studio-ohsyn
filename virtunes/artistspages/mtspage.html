Megan Thee Stallion

Hip-hop, Rap, Trap

Megan Thee Stallion, often referred to as just Meg, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter who has taken the hip-hop world by storm. Known for her fierce and confident persona, Meg delivers powerful verses and catchy hooks in her music. With hits like "Savage" and "Hot Girl Summer," she has gained widespread acclaim and a devoted fan base. Meg's lyrical prowess and unapologetic style have solidified her as one of the leading voices in contemporary rap. With her dynamic flow and undeniable talent, Megan Thee Stallion continues to dominate the music scene and inspire a new generation of artists.
