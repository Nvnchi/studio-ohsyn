Stray Kids

K-POP, Hip-hop, Electronic

Stray Kids is a South Korean boy band known for their dynamic performances and energetic music. With their unique blend of hip-hop, EDM, and pop sounds, Stray Kids has gained a dedicated fan base both in Korea and internationally. The group, consisting of eight members, delivers powerful rap verses, captivating vocals, and impressive choreography. Stray Kids' music often explores themes of youth, self-discovery, and resilience, resonating with a diverse audience. With their infectious energy and strong musicality, Stray Kids continues to make waves in the K-pop industry.
