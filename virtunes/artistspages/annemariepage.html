Anne-Marie

Country, Pop, R&B, Grime

Anne-Marie, born Anne-Marie Nicholson on April 7, 1991, is a British singer-songwriter known for her energetic pop sound and powerful vocals. With her infectious personality and relatable lyrics, Anne-Marie has gained a devoted fan base and achieved chart success. She rose to prominence with hits like "Alarm," "Ciao Adios," and "2002," showcasing her ability to blend catchy melodies with honest and heartfelt storytelling. Anne-Marie's music often explores themes of love, relationships, and self-empowerment, resonating with listeners worldwide. Her dynamic stage presence and engaging performances have further solidified her status as a rising star in the music industry.
