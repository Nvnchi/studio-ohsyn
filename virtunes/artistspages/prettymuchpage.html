PRETTYMUCH

Pop, R&B

PRETTYMUCH is an American-Canadian boy band that emerged on the music scene in 2017. Comprising Austin Porter, Brandon Arreaga, Edwin Honoret, Nick Mara, and Zion Kuwonu, PRETTYMUCH quickly gained a dedicated fan base with their harmonious vocals, charismatic performances, and modern pop-R&B sound. They were formed by Simon Cowell and have been recognized for their impressive dance skills and slick choreography. With their infectious tracks and relatable lyrics, PRETTYMUCH has carved out a niche in the pop music landscape, solidifying their status as a rising force in the industry.
