Charlie Puth

Pop

Charlie Puth, born on December 2, 1991, is an American singer-songwriter and producer. Known for his smooth vocals and catchy pop sound, Puth has made a significant impact on the music industry. He gained widespread recognition with his hit single "See You Again" featuring Wiz Khalifa, which became a tribute song for the "Fast & Furious" film franchise. Puth's music often explores themes of love, relationships, and personal experiences, resonating with listeners on an emotional level. With his melodic hooks and impressive musical skills, Charlie Puth has established himself as a talented and versatile artist.
