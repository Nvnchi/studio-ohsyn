NF

Hip-hop, Christian Hip-hop

NF, born Nathan Feuerstein, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter known for his introspective and emotional music. With his raw and honest lyrics, NF tackles personal struggles and mental health issues in his music. His introspective style, combined with his passionate delivery, has resonated with audiences worldwide. NF's breakout hits like "Let You Down" and "Time" have showcased his ability to create impactful and relatable music. With his powerful storytelling and vulnerability, NF has carved out a unique space in the rap industry, earning him a dedicated fan base and critical acclaim.
