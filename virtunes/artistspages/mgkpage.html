Machine Gun Kelly

Hip-hop, Rap, Rock

Machine Gun Kelly, also known as MGK, is an American rapper, singer, and actor who has made a name for himself with his energetic performances and rebellious attitude. Known for his rapid-fire delivery and emotionally charged lyrics, MGK has gained a loyal following in the hip-hop and rock genres. With hits like "Bad Things" and "Rap Devil," he has showcased his versatility and willingness to push boundaries. Beyond music, MGK has also ventured into acting, with notable roles in films like "The Dirt" and "Bird Box." With his raw talent and captivating stage presence, Machine Gun Kelly continues to leave a lasting impact on the music and entertainment industry.
