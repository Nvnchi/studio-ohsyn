Sam Smith

Pop, R&B, Soul

Sam Smith is a British singer, songwriter, and multi-award-winning artist known for their soulful and emotive vocals. With their captivating voice and heartfelt lyrics, Sam Smith has garnered global recognition and touched the hearts of listeners. Hits like "Stay with Me" and "Too Good at Goodbyes" showcase their powerful range and vulnerability. Beyond their music, Sam Smith has been vocal about LGBTQ+ advocacy and personal experiences, making them a prominent figure in the industry. With their undeniable talent and emotional depth, Sam Smith continues to resonate with audiences worldwide.
