Ice Spice

Hip-hop, Rap

Ice Spice, also known as Isis Naija Gaston, is an American rapper hailing from the Bronx, New York City. Born on January 1, 2000, she embarked on her music career in 2021 after crossing paths with record producer RiotUSA while attending the State University of New York at Purchase. Ice Spice gained significant recognition in late 2022 with her track "Munch (Feelin' U)," which exploded in popularity on TikTok. Building on this success, she continued to release hit singles such as "Bikini Bottom" and "In Ha Mood," leading up to the debut of her extended play, "Like..?" in 2023. Notably, she made her mark on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart with the collaboration "Gangsta Boo" featuring Lil Tjay. Ice Spice also achieved chart success with remix singles like "Princess Diana" (with Nicki Minaj), "Boy's a Liar Pt. 2" (with PinkPantheress), and "Karma" (with Taylor Swift), all of which reached the top five on the Hot 100.
