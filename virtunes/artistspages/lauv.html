LAUV

Pop, Hip-hop, Rap

Lauv, whose real name is Ari Staprans Leff, is an American singer, songwriter, and producer. Known for his introspective and heartfelt music, Lauv has gained widespread recognition for his emotionally-charged lyrics and catchy pop sound. His breakthrough came with the hit single "I Like Me Better" in 2017, which showcased his talent for blending electronic elements with pop sensibilities. Lauv's music often delves into themes of love, self-discovery, and mental health, resonating with a global audience. With his smooth vocals and relatable songwriting, Lauv has established himself as a prominent figure in the contemporary pop music landscape. Additionally, he has collaborated with notable artists such as BTS, Troye Sivan, and Anne-Marie, further expanding his reach and influence in the industry.
