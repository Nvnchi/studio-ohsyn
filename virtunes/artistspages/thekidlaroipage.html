The Kid LAROI

Hip-hop, Rap

The Kid Laroi, whose real name is Charlton Kenneth Jeffrey Howard, is an Australian rapper, singer, and songwriter. He rose to prominence at a young age with his heartfelt and emotionally charged music. The Kid Laroi's style blends elements of rap, hip-hop, and pop, creating a melodic and catchy sound. Known for his introspective lyrics and relatable storytelling, he has garnered a dedicated fanbase worldwide. With his breakout hits like "Without You" and collaborations with industry heavyweights, The Kid Laroi has quickly become a rising star in the music scene.
