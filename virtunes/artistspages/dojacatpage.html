Doja Cat

Hip-Hop, Rap, Pop, Rap

Doja Cat, born Amala Ratna Zandile Dlamini on October 21, 1995, is an American singer, rapper, and songwriter. Known for her versatile musical style and bold personality, Doja Cat has emerged as a prominent figure in the music industry. Her music incorporates elements of pop, R&B, hip-hop, and dance, resulting in catchy and infectious tracks. With her unique blend of clever wordplay, melodic hooks, and captivating visuals, Doja Cat has gained a dedicated fan base and achieved chart success. Her breakthrough hit "Say So" became a viral sensation and propelled her to international stardom. With her fearless creativity and genre-bending approach, Doja Cat continues to push boundaries and leave a lasting impact on the music scene.
