BTS

K-POP, Pop, Dance, Electronic

BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band that has taken the world by storm. Formed in 2013, the group consists of seven members: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. BTS has gained immense global popularity with their infectious music, impressive choreography, and meaningful lyrics. Known for their versatile sound that blends pop, hip-hop, R&B, and other genres, BTS has released numerous chart-topping albums and singles. Their music often addresses themes of youth, self-empowerment, mental health, and societal issues, resonating with a diverse fan base around the globe. BTS has achieved remarkable milestones, including topping the Billboard Hot 100 chart multiple times and selling out stadiums worldwide. BTS has also broken barriers as one of the first K-pop acts to achieve mainstream success and gain a dedicated following worldwide. Their impact on popular culture and music has solidified their status as one of the most influential and beloved acts of the modern era.
