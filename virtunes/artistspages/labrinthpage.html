Labrinth

Dance, Electronic, Pop

Labrinth, born Timothy Lee McKenzie on January 4, 1989, is a British singer, songwriter, and producer known for his diverse musical talents and innovative sound. With a distinct blend of pop, R&B, and electronic elements, Labrinth has established himself as a versatile and boundary-pushing artist. He gained prominence with his solo career, releasing hits like "Beneath Your Beautiful" featuring Emeli Sandé and "Jealous," which showcased his emotive vocals and heartfelt lyrics. Labrinth is also recognized for his work as a producer, collaborating with renowned artists such as Beyoncé, Rihanna, and The Weeknd. His musical contributions have earned him critical acclaim, along with awards such as Ivor Novello and BRIT Awards. Labrinth's ability to craft captivating melodies and thought-provoking lyrics has solidified his status as a respected and innovative figure in the music industry. He also produced the music of the phenomenon series Euphoria.
