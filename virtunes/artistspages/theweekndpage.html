The Weeknd

R&B, Soul, Dance, Electronic

The Weeknd, whose real name is Abel Makkonen Tesfaye, is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and producer. He is known for his distinct falsetto vocals and dark, atmospheric sound. The Weeknd's music combines elements of R&B, pop, and electronic music, creating a unique and captivating style. With his introspective lyrics and catchy melodies, he has achieved significant commercial success and critical acclaim. His breakout album "Trilogy" and subsequent releases like "Starboy" and "After Hours" have solidified his position as one of the most influential artists in contemporary pop and R&B music.
