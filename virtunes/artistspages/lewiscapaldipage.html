Lewis Capaldi

Pop

Lewis Capaldi, born on October 7, 1996, is a Scottish singer-songwriter who has made a profound impact on the music industry with his soulful and emotionally-charged music. With his powerful and raw vocals, Capaldi has captivated listeners around the world. He gained widespread recognition with his breakout hit single "Someone You Loved" in 2019, which showcased his ability to convey heartache and vulnerability through his music. Capaldi's honest and introspective songwriting has resonated deeply with audiences, earning him critical acclaim and numerous awards, including BRIT Awards and Grammy nominations. Known for his self-deprecating humor and genuine personality, Capaldi has become a beloved figure in the music scene, and his heartfelt performances continue to touch the hearts of many.
